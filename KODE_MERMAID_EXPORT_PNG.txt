=== DIAGRAM 2: FLOWCHART ALGORITMA ===

flowchart TD
    Start([Game Start]) --> Init[Initialize Variables]
    Init --> GenSeq[Generate Random Sequence]
    GenSeq --> ShowSeq[Display LED Sequence]
    ShowSeq --> WaitInput[Wait for User Input]
    WaitInput --> ValidateInput{Input Correct?}
    ValidateInput -->|Yes| LevelUp[Level Complete]
    ValidateInput -->|No| GameOver[Game Over]
    LevelUp --> GenSeq
    GameOver --> CalcScore[Calculate Score]
    CalcScore --> SendScore[Send to Server]
    SendScore --> End([End])
    
    style Start fill:#90EE90
    style GameOver fill:#FFB6C1
    style End fill:#DDA0DD
